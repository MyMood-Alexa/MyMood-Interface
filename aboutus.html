MyMood - About Us

System Purpose

My Mood is an interactive Alexa application that would improve, assess, and get help for the user's mental state.

Depression not only has an impact on the individual person, it also has a great impact on the society around them. Unfortunately, depressed individuals tend to have limited knowledge and access to resources regarding their disorder.

The system provides tools for potentially depressed users, including:

  1. Depression alleviation through conversation
  2. Mental health diagnosis by taking an assessment
  3. Location-based suggestion for professional help
In addition, collected data from willing users are sent to UCLA for research.

The ultimate goal of the system is to give depressed users a coping mechanism through conversation, and to give them more knowledge of the available resources that can help them. Submitted research data would be towards a good cause and simultaneously improve the system. By doing so, we hope users will become more aware of their depression by encouraging them be more proactive in curing their depression.
